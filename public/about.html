About Me

I am a graduate from Michigan State University. I majored in Telecommunications: Media and Information, and minored in German. Currently I am 22 years old and living in East Lansing, Michigan.

I like to fashion myself an amateur cook since cooking one of my major passions. You can always find me on the computer checking out new tech, reading a solid book, or in the kitchen cooking up whatever I feel like (I am a massive fan of curries).

Developing for the web is always more enjoyable when you have some really good food to accompany you.

My Passions

Reading

Reading is one of the best ways I have found that lets my creativity flow. I am a massive fan of fiction novels and I own a small library of books where I live. Having entire universes at one's fingertips through reading allows one to really delve into various different themes and viewpoints. My current favorite novel is The Name of the Wind by Patrick Rothfuss.


Technology

Technology was always one of my original hobbies and interests. I grew up on computer games and it turned into a curiosity for everything technological. I've known since I was young that I wanted to turn my love for technology into some sort of career. Designing websites and tinkering with code in my free time is always a good bit of fun.


Cooking

Where would I be without cooking? Cooking has taught me to try new things, it has opened my mind to the boundless resources out in the world. If there is one thing that has taught me the benefit of the concept of iterating things its cooking. It is almost like coding to a degree, you iterate through a recipe with various ingredients until you come to a solid whole that is tasty and worthwhile!
